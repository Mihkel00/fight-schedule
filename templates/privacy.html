Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2024

What We Collect

Fight Schedule uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. We collect:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Device type and browser information
  • Geographic location (city/country level only)
  • Referral sources

We do NOT collect personal information like names, emails, or IP addresses.

Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous traffic analysis
  • Timezone preference: To show fight times in your local timezone

All cookies are anonymous and do not identify you personally.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics processes data on our behalf to provide insights about site usage. Google may use this data in accordance with their own privacy policy. Learn more at Google Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

We display fighter images and fight data from public sources. We do not share your data with any third parties beyond Google Analytics.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Block cookies in your browser settings
  • Opt out of Google Analytics via browser add-on
  • Use private/incognito browsing mode

Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us via our main site.
